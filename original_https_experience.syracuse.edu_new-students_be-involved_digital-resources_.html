Digital Resources

From academic support, to professional development, to health and wellness, Syracuse University offers a multitude of digital resources to support student success.

The platform connects students to over 300 Recognized Student Organizations, their details of how to join and events throughout the academic year.

  • Access: Learn more on the ’Cuse Activities webpage. Please note, you will need your NetID and password.
  • Contact: For additional information or questions, please email Student Engagement or call 315.443.2718.

The Blackboard Learning and Community System hosts courses and important course-related resources such as syllabi, assignments and grades. Faculty and students will have access to Blackboard courses on a nearly continuous basis, as it supports both in-person and virtual classes. Learn more about Blackboard today!

  • Access: To access Blackboard, you will need your NetID and password.
  • Contact: For additional information or questions, please email Information Technology Services or call 315.443.2677.

Degree Works is a web-based tool to help students and advisors monitor a student’s progress toward degree completion. It is designed to facilitate academic advising, but is not intended to replace face-to-face advising. Degree Works combines Syracuse University’s degree requirements and the coursework a student has completed into an easy-to-read worksheet that helps show a student what courses and requirements still need to be completed. Learn more by watching the Degree Works video today!

  • Access: Incoming students can access Degree Works 28 days before the start of class. Learn more on the Accessing Degree Works webpage. Please note, you will need your NetID and password.
  • Contact: For additional information or questions, please visit the Degree Works website, email Information Technology Services or call 315.443.2677.

Handshake is Syracuse University’s centralized career management tool. As a free resource, Handshake hosts available internships, professional employment and professional development opportunities available through ’Cuse Works, alumni and partners.

  • Access: Learn more about accessing Handshake on the Syracuse University Career Services Handshake webpage. Please note, you will need your NetID and password.
  • Contact: For additional information or questions, please email Syracuse University Career Services or call 315.443.3616.

As a mobile app wellness leader and through virtual experiences, Headspace helps to create habits supportive of mental health. Highlights include but are not limited to the following.

  • Learn the fundamentals of meditation and mindfulness directly from experts.
  • Customizable experiences support managing stress, sleep, self-esteem, focus, movement and more.
  • Sleep sounds and bedtime exercises.

Get started today with the following!

  • Access: Learn more on the Headspace instructional webpage.
  • Contact: For additional information or questions, learn more on the Headspace instructional webpage.

MySlice is Syracuse University’s online portal to critical information resources for the campus community. As a student, you will access MySlice to complete your annual Student Health Insurance Plan requirement, apply for residential housing, review your Bursar electronic bill (e-bill), register for Orange Alert and more.

  • Access: Learn more on the Answers MySlice webpage. You will need your NetID and password.
  • Contact: For additional information or questions, please email Information Technology Services or call 315.443.2677.

Fostering personalized support and mentoring, Orange Success is a powerful contributor to the student experience, student success and staying on track for degree completion. The technology platform makes it easy to connect faculty, teaching assistants, academic advisors and staff, to provide and strengthen individual student support.

  • Web-Based, Mobile Friendly
  • Early Alerts, Nurture Improvements, Celebrate Outstanding Work
  • Campus Success Networks, Resources, Services and Support

Get started today with the following!

  • Access: Learn more on the Orange Success webpage. You will need your NetID and password.
  • Contact: For additional information or questions, please email Retention and Student Success or call 315.443.0221.

The Patient Portal helps enhance communications between students and the health and wellness team. This secure and confidential digital portal provides students the opportunity to schedule and access appointments, securely communicate with the health and wellness team, access and submit records such as health statements and immunization history, and more.

  • Access: Learn more on the Patient Portal instructional webpage. Please note, you will need your NetID and password.
  • Contact: For additional information or questions, please email the Barnes Center at The Arch or call 315.443.8000.

Find upcoming events hosted by the campus community on the Syracuse University Community Calendar today!

  • Enter the name of your school or college into the search box to find sponsored events.
  • Explore different categories and event types to help further define your search.

The Wellness Portal is a free and convenient digital platform and mobile app that helps students and campus community members register for health and wellness activities, make reservations and more. Learn how to make a reservation for Fitness Classes, Outdoor Adventure Trips, the Crowley Family MindSpa, the Climbing Wall and more today!

  • Access: Learn more on the Wellness Portal instructional webpage. Please note, you will need your NetID and password.
  • Contact: For additional information or questions, please email the Barnes Center at The Arch or call 315.443.8000.

The Center for International Services hosts International Student Orientation for incoming matriculated undergraduate, graduate and law international students. 

International undergraduate students’ move-in dates for Fall 2025 are Aug. 15-16. Residence halls will be open starting Aug. 15.

If you have any questions, please email the Center for International Services  or call 315.443.2457.

Welcome Ambassadors International

The Welcome Ambassadors International (WAI) are student leaders who are here to support incoming international students in their transition to campus life and a new cultural environment. Students will connect with the WAIs throughout orientation and Welcome Week!

Moving In

  • Home
  • Moving-In – New Student Programs – Syracuse University
In this Section

Fall 2025 Move-In is scheduled for Aug. 18-20 for domestic students. International students may move in Aug. 15-17.

Combining academic convenience and modern amenities, an essential part of your Becoming Orange journey is experiencing the over 20 residence halls hosted throughout North and South Campus. By sharing important information surrounding traveling to campus and move-in dates and times, New Student Programs will guide you through move-in day and settling into your new campus home.

Pharmacy

University pharmacists are happy to answer prescription questions and help you choose the best over-the-counter medication(s) for your needs.

  • Phone Number: 315.443.5691
  • Fax Number: 833.779.5893

Pharmacy Services

Located within the Barnes Center at The Arch and open to students, faculty and staff, the in-person Pharmacy offers the following. Drop-ins are welcome, appointments are not required.

  • Prescription Filling
  • Consultation Services
  • Prescription Counseling
  • Immunization Services
  • In-Person/Virtual Retail Shopping

Pharmacy Order Form

Prescriptions

Prescriptions From an External Provider

If you seek to fill a prescription prescribed by an external provider, such as your home physician, please have your provider fax, phone or electronically prescribe a new prescription to Syracuse University Health Service Pharmacy, 150 Sims Drive, Syracuse, New York, 13244.

Certified as an Electronic Prescriptions for Controlled Substances (EPCS) provider, the Pharmacy is full-service and available to the campus community.

Electronic Prescribing Information

Surescripts Network – Syracuse University Health Service Pharmacy 13244

In accordance with New York State Pharmacy Law, Barnes Center at The Arch Pharmacy staff can only transfer one refill to or from another pharmacy. As requests may increase at times throughout the year and/or your home pharmacy may be in a different time zone, please know the health and wellness team is working to assist students as quickly as possible.

Refills Remaining on Your Prescription

You may take your Barnes Center at The Arch Pharmacy prescription bottle/label to a pharmacy in your area. Your local pharmacy can then call the Barnes Center Pharmacy at 315.443.5691 and request a prescription transfer. Please note, some state laws may prevent this. As a friendly reminder, New York State Pharmacy Law permits the Barnes Center at The Arch Pharmacy to transfer only one refill to another pharmacy.

Out of Prescription Refills

Please contact your provider for a new prescription.

  1. Log into the Wellness Portal.
  2. Select “Virtual Pharmacy.”
  3. Select “Refill a Prescription.”
  4. Complete the form.

If this is your first time using the Wellness Portal, please visit the Wellness Portal webpage for additional information and instruction.

Retail Space (In-Person, Virtual)

Don’t see what you need in the retail space? Let us know by emailing the Pharmacy or calling 315.443.5691.

Frequently purchased retail products are available to order for pick-up. Using the Wellness Portal, select “Virtual Pharmacy,” then select “Order Retail Products.”  Please note, orders may take two to four hours to complete. Individuals will receive a phone call when their order is available. Students, faculty and staff are invited to place orders.

If this is your first time using the Wellness Portal, please visit the Wellness Portal webpage for additional information and instruction.

Available in-person and virtually, the Barnes Center at The Arch Pharmacy maintains a large inventory of common non-prescription items and health supplies. Among hundreds of items in the retail pharmacy, you’ll find the following. For questions or to request a product, please email the Pharmacy or call 315.443.5691.

  • Ear Care Supplies (e.g. ear plugs, earwax remover, etc.)
  • Emergen-C, Vitamin C

  • Essential Cold/Flu Items
  • Eye Care Supplies (e.g. saline solution, contact lens cleaner, eye drops, etc.)
  • First Aid Kits and Supplies (e.g. antibiotic ointment, bandages, cold/hot packs, etc.)
  • Herbal Teas
  • Hand Sanitizer (Locally manufactured by Professor Chris Uyehara.)
  • Hand Sanitizer Wipes
  • Masks (e.g. disposable, reusable)
  • Necessities (e.g. hygiene supplies, toiletries, vitamins, supplements, etc.)

  • Sanitary Items ( e.g. pads, tampons, yeast infection treatment, etc.)

Pharmacy Billing and Fees

The Pharmacy works to keep prices affordable, resulting in many prescription and non-prescription items being significantly cheaper compared to traditional retailers. The Pharmacy participates with most insurance prescription plans, including the Student Health Insurance Plan. If a copay and/or fee is required past insurance processing, the Pharmacy accepts the following forms of payment.

  • Credit Cards (MasterCard, Visa, Discover)
  • Billing the student’s Bursar’s account.

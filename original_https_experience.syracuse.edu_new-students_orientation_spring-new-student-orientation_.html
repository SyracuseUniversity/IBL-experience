Spring New Student Orientation

  • Home
  • Orientation – New Student Programs – Syracuse University
  • Spring New Student Orientation

Spring New Student Orientation (Spring NSO) is Syracuse University’s required orientation program for students new to campus in the spring semester. This includes transfer, first-year and Discovery students new to campus in spring 2025. For questions or to request accommodations, please contact the New Student Programs team.

Spring NSO Dates: January 10-12, 2025

Move-In Dates:

  • International and Exchange Students may move-in beginning January 9, 2025

  • Domestic and Discovery students move-in: January 10, 2025

Explore the Spring NSO schedule below. Students, parents, families and supporters will receive a detailed schedule upon checking in at the Check-In and Resource Fair.

Audience Key:

  • D = Discovery
  • I = International
  • FY = First-Year
  • T = Transfer
  • V = Veteran and Military-Connected
  • PFS = Parents, Families and Supporters
  • ? = Otto will be in attendance!

Friday, Jan. 10, 2025

Saturday, Jan. 11, 2025

Sunday, Jan. 12, 2025

Parents, Families and Supporters

Parents, Families and Supporters joining their student during New Student Orientation are invited to attend select sessions. All parents, families and supporters are encouraged to leave campus by Noon, Saturday, January 11, 2025.

Campus Hours of Operation

Campus offices will be open during New Student Orientation (January 10-12, 2025) to accommodate the needs of new and returning students. All offices are open on Friday, Jan. 10, 2025 from 8:30 a.m.-5 p.m. Saturday, Jan. 11 and Sunday, Jan. 12 hours are below.

Within the “Resource Name and Website” column, select a Resource to view their website. You will see the address/location at the bottom of each webpage.

In the event of severe weather and the University switches to essential staff only, academic advising, and New Student Programs activities will switch to remote services. Notice of schedule changes will be listed on the New Student Programs website as well as distributed via Orange Alert. Sign up for Orange Alert today! More details coming soon!
